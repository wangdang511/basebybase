Description: Base-By-Base is a whole genome pairwise and multiple alignment editor. Base-By-Base hilights differences between pairs of alignments and allows the user to easily navigate large alignemts of similar sequences. Although Base-By-Base was intended as an editor and viewer for alignmets of highly similar sequences, it is also provides many of the functions of other generic alignment editors.
